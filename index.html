About Me

Welcome! I’m happy you’re here. You can call me Kate, which is one of many nicknames, or Kathryne, which is my full first name. Either way, I’m a self-taught cooker from Bangladesh. I started this blog in 2020 and named it after my food-network. Now, we live in Dhaka City and work on this blog full-time. I love my job!
